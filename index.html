Play Frogger

Instructions

How to play:

By using the up/down arrow keys or w/s keys, you can move the frog up and down.

Space bar will make the frog jump.

Do not touch the cars or you will lose a life!

When you reach the water, jump on the logs or you will drown and lose a life!

You only have 3 lives so use them wisely!

Get to the green pasture on the right side of the screen to advance to the next level!

To restart the game simply refresh the page.

GOOD LUCK!
